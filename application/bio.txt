Kangkook Jee is a Ph.D candidate in Computer Science at Columbia University.
His research interest centers around the area of system security with focus on
on building secure and reliable software systems leveraging virtual machine
introspection, static and dynamic program analysis.

Prior to his years in the doctoral program, he spent five years from industry
working for IBM Korea.  As a software engineer, he supported AIX operating
system, Java Virtual Machine and IBM's high availability solutions.
