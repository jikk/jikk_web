Kangkook Jee is a Ph.D candidate in Computer Science at Columbia University.
His research interest is centered around the area of system security focusing
on building secure and reliable software systems leveraging innovations of
virtual machine introspection, reverse engineering, and static program analysis.

He also has five years of industry experience working for IBM Korea prior to
doctoral program. As a software engineer, he supported AIX operating system,
Java Virtual Machine and IBM's high availability solutions.
